Who are we?

We are the Northfield Linux User Group (NORLUG). We are a group of Technology and Linux enthusiasts and advocators from the Northfield, MN area.

What do we do?

We host weekly social hack nights at local coffee shops (coffee house coders). We also organize and host technical, Linux focused meetings. Please see the above links for more information on each of those events.

How do I join?

Membership is free! All that is required is a love for technology. Subscribe to our mailing list. Say hello on IRC. Join our Google+ community or follow us on Twitter!
